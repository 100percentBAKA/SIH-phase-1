A day after Supreme Court's verdict wherein it refused to legalise same-sex marriage, a gay couple got engaged in front of the apex court. "Yesterday hurt. Today,@utkarsh__saxena and I went back to the court that denied our rights...and exchanged rings," wrote Ananya Kotia. Lawyer Utkarsh Saxena and his partner Ananya Kotia had filed petition seeking legalisation of same-sex marriage.
