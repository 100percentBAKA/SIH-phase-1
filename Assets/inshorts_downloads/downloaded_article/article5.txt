Mayor Eric Adams hosted Diwali celebrations at his New York residence and called the festival "more than just a holiday". "Let's be better human beings. Let's live in the spirit of Diwali. Let's live in the spirit of [Mahatma] Gandhi...[Goddess] Sita...[and Lord] Ram," Adams said. More than lighting a candle, Diwali is about lighting up our lives, he added.
