The Indian Railways approved four pairs of new trains in South Eastern Railway jurisdiction with three of them serving Odisha's Mayurbhanj district, predominantly inhabited by tribal communities. This is the first time that the native place of President Droupadi Murmu in Rairangpur and Badampahar will have passenger mail/express connectivity.
