After nearly 500 people were reportedly killed in an airstrike on a hospital in Gaza, PM Narendra Modi said he was shocked by the tragic loss of lives. "Civilian casualties in the ongoing conflict are a matter of serious and continuing concern. Those involved should be held responsible," he posted on X. Israel blamed outfit Islamic Jihad for the attack.
