Squadron Cadet Captain Pratham Mahale of the National Defence Academy (NDA) in Maharashtra passed away on Wednesday after sustaining a head injury during an inter-squadron boxing competition. He underwent surgery for internal bleeding but could not be revived despite "best efforts", the NDA said. Notably, the deceased was set to pass out of the academy by the end of November.
