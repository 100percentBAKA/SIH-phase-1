Swati Dubey
After Delhi HC halted the eviction of Raghav Chadha from the government bungalow on Tuesday, the AAP MP shared a video message hailing the decision and said, "Truth won and lies lost." He added, "The leaders of non-BJP...parties are being...targeted...BJP...might evict me from my house and even...Rajya Sabha, but not from the hearts of millions of people."
