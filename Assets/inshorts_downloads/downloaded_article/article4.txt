A video has surfaced online showing women from Gujarat's Rajkot performing garba on bikes and cars with swords in their hands. In the video, multiple women clad in traditional outfits were also seen performing stunts on cars and scooters with swords in their hands. According to reports, the performance took place on Tuesday.
