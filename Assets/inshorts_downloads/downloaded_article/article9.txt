Shalini Ojha
A 21-year-old fourth-year student of IIT-Kharagpur, identified as K Kiran Chandra, was found dead in a hostel room on Tuesday in a suspected case of suicide. "He was a boarder of the Lal Bahadur Shastri (LBS) Hall of Residence and chose the path of self-harm," IIT-Kharagpur said in a statement. Police registered a case of unnatural death.
