After India evacuated 18 Nepalis from war-torn Israel under 'Operation Ajay', Nepal Foreign Minister NP Saud said, "We appreciate this help in difficult time. Nepal and Nepali people are thankful." Thanking the Indian government, a Nepali evacuee said, "The situation in Israel is dangerous. We were scared. There were explosions." Flights are also being sent from Nepal to evacuate Nepalis.
